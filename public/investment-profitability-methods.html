4.2 Accounting Rate of Return (ARR)

NI = sum(NI_i)/(N-1)
BV = sum(BV_i)/(N-1)
r = NI / BV

4.3 Net Present Value (NPV)

NPV = sum(NCF_t / (1+r)^t)

4.4 Internal Rate of Return (IRR)

sum(NCF_t / (1+IRR)^t) = 0

4.5 Investment Risk
