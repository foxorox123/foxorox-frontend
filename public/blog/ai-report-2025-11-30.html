Why Falling Inflation Can Hurt Retailers

  • Price-led revenue growth fades — volume must replace inflation-driven price effects.
  • Harder year-on-year comparisons after boosted results in high inflation cycles.
  • Competition returns to pricing and promotions, compressing margins.
  • Wages remain sticky even as inflation cools.
  • Fashion exposure increases inventory risk without inflation as a margin cushion.

This explains why short-term signals for Polish retail equities may turn bearish even though macro headlines look positive.
